## Data boundary

The pilot may use only information required to operate lead and quote follow-up, such as:

- business/customer name;
- business contact details;
- enquiry or quote reference;
- service requested;
- quote value;
- status;
- owner;
- next action;
- due date;
- communication outcome.

Do not import:

- identity documents;
- banking credentials;
- card details;
- passwords or API secrets;
- medical or health information;
- information about children;
- criminal, biometric or similarly sensitive records;
- full email or WhatsApp histories when a short operational summary is sufficient.

## Client authorisation

The client confirms that:

1. it is authorised to provide the records used in this pilot;
2. the records are supplied for the purpose of organising and operating the agreed Sales Admin workflow;
3. access is limited to authorised client users and named TAD operators;
4. TAD will not automatically send messages, make payments or change external accounts without explicit authority;
5. important decisions will be routed to the client through the Service Desk;
6. either party may stop the pilot;
7. the client will identify records that must be removed or corrected;
8. pilot reporting may use anonymised aggregate results, but not client-identifying information without separate permission.
