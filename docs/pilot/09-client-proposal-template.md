## Recommended engagement

The Admin Department will install and operate a Sales Admin workflow using DueToday as the shared control platform.

The engagement covers:

1. confirmation of scope and data authority;
2. cleanup and import of the agreed active records;
3. workflow, statuses, owners and next-action rules;
4. client and operator access;
5. daily managed queue operation during stabilisation;
6. human-approved decisions through the Service Desk;
7. weekly evidence and a continue/change/stop review.

## Deliverables

- configured managed workspace;
- installed Sales Admin workflow;
- up to 50 cleaned active records;
- owner, next action and due date on active records;
- follow-up templates;
- approval process;
- baseline scorecard;
- two weekly reports during the 14-day stabilisation period;
- closeout recommendation.

## Client responsibilities

The client will:

- confirm authority to provide the records;
- nominate an owner or manager for decisions;
- verify quote values, commitments and customer information;
- respond to approvals within the agreed time;
- identify data that must be corrected or removed;
- avoid sharing passwords, banking credentials or unnecessary sensitive data.

## Boundaries

This engagement does not include autonomous customer messaging, payment execution, legal advice, debt collection, accounting replacement or changes to external accounts without explicit authority.
